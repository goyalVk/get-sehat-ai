## Hemoglobin Kya Hota Hai?

Hemoglobin (Hb) woh protein hai jo aapke red blood cells mein hota hai. Iska ek hi kaam hai — oxygen ko lungs se lekar body ke har hisse tak pohanchana. Jab yeh level normal se neeche girti hai, toh uss condition ko **anemia** ya **khoon ki kami** kehte hain.

India mein anemia bahut common problem hai — khaas kar mahilaon aur bachchon mein.

## Normal Hemoglobin Level Kya Hona Chahiye?

| Group | Normal Range |
|-------|-------------|
| Adult Mard | 13.5 – 17.5 g/dL |
| Adult Aurat | 12.0 – 15.5 g/dL |
| Pregnant Aurat | 11.0 g/dL se upar |
| Bachche (6-12 saal) | 11.5 – 15.5 g/dL |

Agar aapki report mein Hb is range se neeche hai, toh doctor se milna zaroori hai.

## Hemoglobin Kam Hone ke Symptoms

Kai log anemia ke signs ko "thakaan" samajh kar ignore kar dete hain. Lekin yeh symptoms dhyan se observe karo:

**Sabse common signs:**
- **Bahut zyada thakaan aur kamzori** — thoda kaam karo aur ekdum thak jaate ho
- **Saanson ka phooltna** — seedhi chaadte waqt ya thodi si exercise mein hi haanfne lagana
- **Chehre ka pila padna** — aankhon ke andar ki laal lining bhi peeli dikhe, nakhun pale ho jaayein
- **Baar baar sir dard aur chakkar aana**
- **Dil ki dhadkan tez hona** — heart zyada mehnat karta hai oxygen pohanchane ke liye
- **Haath-paon thande rehna** — khoon ka circulation slow hone ki wajah se
- **Concentration mein dikkat** — padhna ya kaam karna mushkil lagta hai

Bachon mein additional signs ho sakte hain jaise growth slow hona ya school mein performance kharaab hona.

## Hemoglobin Kam Kyun Hoti Hai?

**1. Iron ki kami (Sabse common reason India mein)**

Iron se hi hemoglobin banta hai. Iron ki kami hogi toh Hb bhi gir jaayegi. Yeh hota hai jab:
- Khane mein iron-rich foods kam hain
- Periods mein bohot zyada blood loss ho raha hai
- Gut mein iron absorb nahi ho raha (malabsorption)

**2. Vitamin B12 ki kami**

B12 red blood cells ke formation mein help karta hai. Vegetarians aur vegans mein yeh zyada common hai kyunki B12 mostly animal foods mein hoti hai — eggs, milk, meat, fish.

**3. Folic Acid ki kami**

Folic acid bhi blood cells ke liye zaroori hai. Pregnancy mein agar folic acid kam ho toh baby ke development mein bhi problem aa sakti hai.

**4. Chronic diseases**

Kidney disease, liver problems, ya chronic inflammation bhi Hb ko low kar sakti hain. Iss case mein sirf diet se fix nahi hoga.

**5. Zyada blood loss**

Heavy periods (menorrhagia), koi injury, surgery, ya internal bleeding se bhi anemia ho sakti hai.

**6. Genetic conditions**

Thalassemia aur Sickle Cell Anemia inherited conditions hain jo India mein bohot common hain — khaas kar Gujarat, Maharashtra, aur eastern states mein.

## Kya Khaayein — Iron-Rich Foods List

**Iron ke best sources:**
- Palak, methi, chaulai (amaranth), bathua
- Rajma, chana, masoor dal, moong dal
- Tofu, paneer
- Eggs, chicken, fish (especially liver)
- Dry fruits — khajoori, anjeer, kismis
- Til, kaju

**Pro tip:** Iron-rich foods ke saath Vitamin C lena iron absorption badhata hai. Toh khane mein nimbu, amla, ya orange ka juice lein. Dal ke saath nimbu squeeze karo — yeh ek easy trick hai.

**Kya avoid karein:**
- Chai aur coffee — khane ke turant baad mat peeyein, iron absorption rokti hain
- Calcium-rich cheezein (milk, curd) aur iron-rich foods ek saath mat lein
- Antacids aur iron supplements ek saath nahi

## Vitamin B12 aur Folate ke liye Kya Khaayein?

**B12 ke liye:** Doodh, dahi, paneer, eggs, chicken, fish. Strict vegans ko doctor se supplements ke baare mein poochhna chahiye.

**Folic acid ke liye:** Hari sabziyan (palak, broccoli, methi), rajma, chana, moong, kela, papaya.

## Doctor Kab Dikhayein?

- Hb 10 g/dL se neeche ho gayi ho
- Symptoms bahut zyada hain ya daily life affect ho rahi hai
- Pregnant hain aur Hb 11 g/dL se neeche hai
- Bachche mein symptoms dikh rahe hain
- Pehle se iron supplements le rahe hain fir bhi improvement nahi

Sirf diet se severe anemia theek nahi hoti — doctor iron supplements, B12 injections, ya dusre treatment suggest kar sakte hain. Self-medication mat karo.

## Summary

Hemoglobin kam hona common hai lekin serious bhi ho sakta hai. Thakaan, pila chehra, aur saanson ka phooltna — yeh sab ignore mat karo. Ek simple blood test (CBC) se pata chal jaata hai. Khaan-paan theek karo, doctor ki salah lo, aur regular follow-up rako.
