## CBC Kya Hota Hai?

CBC ka full form hai **Complete Blood Count** — isse **Hemogram** bhi kehte hain. Yeh ek basic lekin bahut important blood test hai jo aapke blood ke teen main components measure karta hai:

1. **Red Blood Cells (RBC)** — Oxygen pohanchate hain
2. **White Blood Cells (WBC)** — Infection se ladte hain
3. **Platelets** — Khoon band karte hain (clotting)

Ek CBC report mein in sab ke alag alag numbers hote hain. Aao samajhte hain har ek ko:

---

## 1. Hemoglobin (Hb)

Yeh RBC mein hota hai aur oxygen carry karta hai.

| Group | Normal Range |
|-------|-------------|
| Mard | 13.5 – 17.5 g/dL |
| Aurat | 12.0 – 15.5 g/dL |

**Kam hone par (Anemia):** Thakaan, chakkar, pila chehra
**Zyada hone par:** Dehydration ya certain blood disorders

---

## 2. RBC Count (Red Blood Cell Count)

Khoon mein total red blood cells ka count.

| Group | Normal Range |
|-------|-------------|
| Mard | 4.5 – 5.9 million/µL |
| Aurat | 4.1 – 5.1 million/µL |

Agar RBC count kam hai aur Hb bhi kam hai — toh clearly anemia hai.

---

## 3. MCV (Mean Corpuscular Volume)

Ek RBC cell kitni badi hai. Ismein anemia ka **type** pata chalta hai.

**Normal:** 80 – 100 fL

- **MCV kam (Microcytic):** Iron deficiency anemia ya thalassemia
- **MCV normal:** Anemia of chronic disease ya acute blood loss
- **MCV zyada (Macrocytic):** Vitamin B12 ya folic acid ki kami

Yeh ek detective clue hai doctor ke liye — anemia kyun ho rahi hai.

---

## 4. MCH aur MCHC

**MCH (Mean Corpuscular Hemoglobin):** Ek RBC mein average kitna hemoglobin hai.
- Normal: 27 – 32 pg

**MCHC (Mean Corpuscular Hemoglobin Concentration):** RBC mein hemoglobin ki concentration.
- Normal: 32 – 36 g/dL

Dono low hona usually iron deficiency indicate karta hai. Yeh values MCV ke saath milake dekhi jaati hain.

---

## 5. Hematocrit (PCV — Packed Cell Volume)

Blood mein RBCs ka percentage.

| Group | Normal Range |
|-------|-------------|
| Mard | 41% – 53% |
| Aurat | 36% – 46% |

Anemia mein yeh kam hota hai, dehydration mein zyada.

---

## 6. WBC Count (White Blood Cell Count)

Yeh infection-fighting cells hain. Inki count se infection, inflammation, ya immune disorders ka pata chalta hai.

**Normal range:** 4,000 – 11,000 cells/µL (ya 4.0 – 11.0 × 10³/µL)

**WBC zyada (Leukocytosis):**
- Bacterial infection (most common reason)
- Inflammation ya injury
- Steroid medicines
- Kuch cancers (leukemia) — lekin yeh rare hai

**WBC kam (Leukopenia):**
- Viral infections (dengue, typhoid, etc.)
- Bone marrow problems
- Certain medications
- Autoimmune conditions

---

## 7. WBC Differential Count

CBC report mein WBC ke bhi types hote hain. Inhe collectively **differential count** kehte hain:

| Type | Normal % | Kaam |
|------|----------|------|
| Neutrophils | 50–70% | Bacterial infection se ladte hain |
| Lymphocytes | 20–40% | Viral infection se ladte hain |
| Monocytes | 2–8% | Chronic infections mein |
| Eosinophils | 1–4% | Allergy aur parasites ke against |
| Basophils | 0–1% | Inflammatory response |

**Neutrophils zyada:** Bacterial infection
**Lymphocytes zyada:** Viral infection (common cold, dengue, etc.)
**Eosinophils zyada:** Allergy ya worm infestation — India mein common

---

## 8. Platelet Count

Platelets woh tiny cells hain jo khoon band karne mein help karte hain — cut lagne par ya andar kahin bleeding ho toh.

**Normal range:** 1.5 – 4.0 lakh/µL (ya 150,000 – 400,000/µL)

**Platelets kam (Thrombocytopenia):**
- Dengue fever — India mein yeh bahut common hai, dengue mein platelets quickly girte hain
- Viral infections
- Certain medicines
- Bone marrow disorders

**Platelets zyada (Thrombocytosis):**
- Usually infection ya iron deficiency mein
- Kuch cases mein blood disorders mein bhi

**Alert:** Agar platelets 50,000 se neeche aa jaayein, toh bleeding ka risk badh jaata hai. Immediate medical attention zaroor lein.

---

## 9. RDW (Red Cell Distribution Width)

Yeh batata hai ki aapke red blood cells size mein kitne alag-alag hain.

**Normal:** 11.5% – 14.5%

High RDW matlab alag-alag size ki RBCs hain — iron deficiency ya B12 deficiency ka indicator ho sakta hai.

---

## CBC Report Mein Kya Dekhen?

Jab aap apni CBC report dekhein, yeh 4 cheezein dhyan se dekho:

1. **Hemoglobin** — anemia check karne ke liye
2. **WBC count** — infection ya immunity check
3. **Platelets** — clotting ability
4. **MCV** — anemia ka type samajhne ke liye

Har value ke saath ek "Reference Range" diya hota hai — bas apni value usse compare karo.

## Doctor Kab Dikhayein?

- Hb 10 g/dL se neeche
- WBC 3,000 se neeche ya 20,000 se upar
- Platelets 100,000 se neeche
- Koi bhi value bahut alag normal se

Sirf ek value abnormal ho toh ghabrao mat — doctor context dekhte hain. Lekin clearly abnormal values ko ignore nahi karna chahiye.
