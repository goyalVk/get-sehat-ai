## Thyroid Gland Kya Hota Hai?

Thyroid ek butterfly-shaped gland hai jo aapki gaardhan (neck) mein hoti hai. Yeh hormones banati hai — **T3 (Triiodothyronine)** aur **T4 (Thyroxine)** — jo body ki metabolism control karte hain. Matlab body kitni energy use karti hai, heart kitni tez dhakdhakata hai, body ka temperature, weight — sab kuch thyroid se connected hai.

**TSH (Thyroid Stimulating Hormone)** actually thyroid gland ka hormone nahi — yeh **pituitary gland** banati hai brain mein. TSH ka kaam hai thyroid ko batana kitna hormone banana hai. Isliye TSH se hume pata chalta hai thyroid sahi kaam kar rahi hai ya nahi.

---

## TSH Normal Range

| Result | Matlab |
|--------|--------|
| 0.4 – 4.0 mIU/L | Normal — thyroid theek hai |
| 4.0 – 10.0 mIU/L | Mildly high — possible hypothyroidism |
| 10.0 se upar | Clearly hypothyroidism |
| 0.1 se neeche | Hyperthyroidism (overactive thyroid) |

**Important:** Har lab ki range thodi alag ho sakti hai. Hamesha apni report ki reference range dekho.

**Pregnancy mein** ranges alag hoti hain — trimester ke hisaab se TSH ki acceptable limit change hoti hai. Pregnant women doctors se specific guidance lein.

---

## TSH Ka Seedha Logic

- **TSH zyada = Thyroid slow hai (Hypothyroidism)**
  Brain zyada TSH bhej raha hai kyunki thyroid ko "kick" dena hai

- **TSH kam = Thyroid zyada active hai (Hyperthyroidism)**
  Brain TSH kam kar raha hai kyunki thyroid already bahut zyada hormone bana rahi hai

Yeh ek feedback loop hai — jab thyroid hormone kam hoga, pituitary zyada TSH release karega. Aur jab hormone zyada hoga, TSH kam ho jaayega.

---

## Hypothyroidism — Thyroid Ka Slow Hona

**TSH zyada + T4 kam = Hypothyroidism**

Yeh India mein bahut common hai — khaas kar mahilaon mein. Iodine ki kami bhi ek major cause hai.

**Symptoms:**

- **Bahut zyada thakaan** — poori neend ke baad bhi thaka hua feel karna
- **Weight badh raha hai** bina khaana zyada khaye
- **Thandi zyada lagti hai** — jab dusron ko nahi
- **Baal jhadna** — kaafi zyada
- **Skin dry aur rough** ho jaana
- **Constipation** — pet saaf nahi hota
- **Yaaddasht kamzor** — koi baat yaad nahi rehti
- **Depression ya low mood**
- **Periods irregular** (mahilaon mein)
- **Chehra phoola hua lagta hai** — khaas kar aankhen ke neeche
- **Awaaz bhaari ho jaana**

**Causes:**
- Hashimoto's thyroiditis (autoimmune — immune system thyroid ko attack karta hai)
- Iodine ki kami (namak mein iodized salt use karo)
- Radiation therapy
- Certain medicines (lithium, amiodarone)

---

## Hyperthyroidism — Thyroid Ka Zyada Active Hona

**TSH kam + T4 zyada = Hyperthyroidism**

Hypothyroidism se kam common hai lekin zyada troublesome symptoms hote hain.

**Symptoms:**

- **Weight ghatta hai** bina diet ke
- **Dil ki dhadkan tez** ya irregular (palpitations)
- **Bahut zyada garmi lagti hai** aur pasina aata hai
- **Haath kaanpna** (tremors)
- **Neend nahi aati** — anxiety rehti hai
- **Bowel movements zyada** — diarrhea tendency
- **Aankhein bahar aane lagti hain** (Graves' disease mein)
- **Muscles kamzor** lagti hain
- **Zyada bhook lagti hai** lekin weight nahi badhta

**Main causes:**
- Graves' disease (autoimmune — most common)
- Thyroid nodules (toxic nodules)
- Thyroiditis (temporary inflammation)
- Zyada iodine ya thyroid medicine

---

## T3 aur T4 Bhi Report Mein Hote Hain

Aksar doctor TSH ke saath T3 aur T4 bhi test karte hain:

**Free T4 (fT4):**
- Normal: 0.8 – 1.8 ng/dL
- Yeh active thyroid hormone hai
- TSH ke saath milake dekha jaata hai

**Free T3 (fT3):**
- Normal: 2.3 – 4.2 pg/mL
- T4 body mein convert hota hai T3 mein

**TSH high + T4 low** = Confirmed hypothyroidism — treatment usually shuru hoti hai
**TSH high + T4 normal** = Subclinical hypothyroidism — watch karte hain

---

## Treatment Kya Hoti Hai?

**Hypothyroidism:**
- **Levothyroxine** (Eltroxin, Thyronorm) — yeh most common thyroid medicine hai India mein
- Roz subah khaali pet leni hoti hai
- Regular blood test se dose adjust hota hai
- Usually lifelong leni padti hai

**Hyperthyroidism:**
- Anti-thyroid medicines (Methimazole, PTU)
- Radioactive iodine therapy
- Surgery (thyroidectomy) — severe cases mein

---

## Thyroid Test Kitni Baar Karwayein?

- **Normal log (no symptoms):** 40 saal ke baad, khaas kar mahilaon ko ek baar check karwana chahiye
- **Family history hai:** 35 saal se shuru karo
- **Symptoms hain:** Immediately check karwao
- **Already thyroid problem hai:** Doctor ke bolne par — usually har 6-12 mahine

---

## Summary

TSH test thyroid function ka best indicator hai. 0.4-4.0 mIU/L normal range hai. TSH zyada toh thyroid slow (hypothyroidism) — thakaan, weight gain, thandi; TSH kam toh thyroid fast (hyperthyroidism) — weight loss, palpitations, anxiety. Dono treatable hain, lekin regular monitoring zaroori hai.
